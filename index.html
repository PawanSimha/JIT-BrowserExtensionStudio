About

What is JIT?

JIT is a small studio that builds clean, focused browser extensions with zero data collection. Every tool we ship is crafted with the same principles: do one thing well, respect the user, and stay out of the way. All extensions are free, open source, and require no account or sign-up.

What are JIT's core principles?

  • ◎
    Privacy first.

    Zero data leaves your browser. No analytics, no trackers, no servers.

  • ◇
    Open source.

    Every extension is transparent, auditable, and free forever.

  • ◈
    Quietly powerful.

    Small tools that fit naturally into how you already work.

Extensions

What tools does JIT offer?

JIT offers four free Chrome extensions with more in development. MutedHue replaces harsh blue selection with adaptive grey, Refreshner auto-refreshes pages and monitors keywords, and Goofanizer emulates mobile and tablet viewports. All four are open source and collect zero data.

MutedHue

Published

Replaces harsh blue text selection with a subtle, adaptive grey highlight on every website. Gentle on the eyes, beautiful on every theme.

Learn more ›

Refreshner

Published

Smart auto-refresh with customizable intervals, keyword page monitoring, live countdown timer, and audio alerts when matches are found.

Learn more ›

Goofanizer

Published

Instantly emulate mobile and tablet viewports with one click. Switch between 4 predefined device presets, rotate, capture screenshots, and batch export.

Learn more ›

Imageination

Published

Scans any webpage for images, video and audio. Download PNG, JPEG, WebP, SVG, GIF, AVIF, ICO, MP4, WebM, MP3 and more individually or batch as ZIP.
