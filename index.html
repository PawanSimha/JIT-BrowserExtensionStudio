About

What JIT stands for

JIT is a small studio that builds clean, focused browser extensions. Every tool we ship is crafted with the same principles: do one thing well, respect the user, and stay out of the way.

  • ◎
    Privacy first.

    Zero data leaves your browser. No analytics, no trackers, no servers.

  • ◇
    Open source.

    Every extension is transparent, auditable, and free forever.

  • ◈
    Quietly powerful.

    Small tools that fit naturally into how you already work.
